Pontiac G8 GXP, Fast and Family Oriented

It's Background

The base G8 was produced in Australia as the Holden Commodore and rebaged/sold within the United States by Pontiac as the G8.

The Specs

The G8 GXP was produced with an amazing 6.2 liter, Cheverolet Corvette LS3 V8 engine which can push 415 hp and 415 lb-ft of torque. It can go from 0 to 60 in 4.5 seconds!

Fast and Family Oriented

This awesome vehicle isn't only fast, it's also a four-door sedan which can accomodate a small family of four. This is amazing for those dads that want to drop-off their sleepy kids who missed the bus in the morning in a very stylish and speedy fashion!
